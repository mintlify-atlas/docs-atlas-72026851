Movie Circle lets you discover trending and popular movies and TV shows, organize them into named collections, annotate each entry with a personal rating and notes, and share your lists with others via a simple link. Built on Next.js and powered by The Movie Database (TMDB), it combines a full browsing experience with a lightweight social layer.

## What you can do

Movie Circle is organized around three core ideas:

- **Discover** — Browse popular and trending movies and TV shows sourced from TMDB, with full detail pages showing cast, reviews, keywords, trailers, and related content.
- **Collect** — Create named collections (like "Watchlist" or "Favorites") and add any movie or TV show to them with a personal rating and notes. Every new account gets three default collections — **To Watch**, **Watching**, and **Watched** — to track your viewing status automatically.
- **Share** — Share any collection or individual media entry via a public link. Add password protection or restrict access to specific email addresses.

## Tech overview

Movie Circle is a single Next.js application using the App Router. The backend is PostgreSQL via Prisma ORM. Authentication is handled by NextAuth v5 with support for email/password, GitHub, and Google sign-in. TMDB provides all movie and TV metadata.
